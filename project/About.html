    Who we are

    An independent voice in international claims management.

    Equijust was founded in 1996 with a simple conviction: that complex claims deserve specialists who understand both the technical detail and the human context behind every loss. Nearly thirty years on, that conviction still shapes how we work.

    We are a firm of loss adjusters focused on financial lines, professional risks, commercial crime, cyber and specialty insurance. From our offices in London, Miami and Bogotá — and through a trusted network of partners across the globe — we handle claims that span jurisdictions, languages and legal systems.

    Independence matters to us. It is what lets us give insurers, reinsurers and their clients an honest technical view, free from conflict and built on experience earned case by case.
